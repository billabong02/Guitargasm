All-American Rejects Rock NASCAR’s Chevy Rock & Roll 400

Multi-platinum recording artists The All-American Rejects will make a pit stop from their worldwide tour at Richmond International Raceway on Saturday, September 12 to perform hits from their third album “When the World Comes Down.” The band was recently selected to perform trackside during pre-race festivities for NASCAR’s Chevy Rock & Roll 400.

In addition to sharing track time, albeit playing music on track...not driving, the band will have one more thing in common with the race’s winner. The All-American Rejects’ guitarist Mike Kennerty will be playing a new Mira X model PRS guitar during their pre-race concert, and the winner of the Chevy Rock & Roll 400 will win their very own custom built PRS guitar. The custom guitar, hand crafted at PRS, has been painted to match the pace car and is the centerpiece of the custom Rock and Roll themed race trophy created by PRS Guitars.
